So who exactly is Gregorio del Pilar?
Here is a quick introduction of our Batang Heneral before we dig deeper into details about his life!
Gregorio del Pilar, also known as "Goyo" or "Goyong" is certainly not the youngest general there is to exist, but he is considered as one of the youngest generals in the Philippine revolutionary Forces. He was born on November 14, 1875 and died at the age 24, December 2, 1899 for the sake of protecting and covering his fellow warriors and Filipinos. His last love was identified to be Remedios Nable Jose, however, this was opposed by some people as they had believed that Dolores, the sister of Remedios, is his actual last love.
